About Enactus

Enactus is all about social entrepreneurship and creating socially sensitive leaders. We are a global non-profit organisation and community of students, academics and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and creat a better, more sustainable world.

About Enactus USS

Enactus USS is represented by the students of GGS IP University's Main Campus and is one of the most prestigious colleges in Delhi. We have come a long way in our journey with major achievements in our projects and have been competing in nationals for the past 2 years. We have received funding by big companies like KPMG and Blue Dart who have seen potential in our work. We recently have won the KPMG Business Ethics Grant 2018 for our work which got us further more funding for our projects.
