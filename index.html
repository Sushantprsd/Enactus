We are the social entreprenuership society of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. Enactus USS was established in 2015 with the vision of creating sustainable business opportunities for communities that have potential but lack resources. With the help of our faculty and alumni, we started two projects namely Project Aakar and Project Ankuran.
